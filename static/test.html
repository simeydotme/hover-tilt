Usage Example

Here's how to use the component in your HTML:

<!-- Import the component -->
<script type="module" src="../dist/index.js"></script>

<!-- Use the component -->
<hover-tilt tilt-factor="1.5" scale-factor="1.1" glare-intensity="1.5">
	<div style="width: 300px; aspect-ratio: 0.7159; background: #667eea;">
		Your content here
	</div>
</hover-tilt>
